## Draft 3 scope statement

Draft 3 adds support for self-informing Duotronic systems. Chronological personal/project/system evidence can become governed witness material. High-speed lookup memory can support internal reasoning. Diverse models can be adjudicated as evidence sources. Planners can propose actions as candidate witnesses. Actions execute only after policy gates and produce outcome witnesses.
